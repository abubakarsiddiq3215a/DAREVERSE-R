How DareVerse Works
The Basics
  • Create a Challenge: Dare your friends with fun tasks. Tag them to send the challenge.
  • Submit Proof: Complete a dare and upload a photo or video as evidence (up to 10MB, auto-compressed).
  • Verify Proofs: As a challenge creator, you review and approve or reject your friends' submissions.
  • Earn Points: Get ranked points for completing challenges, creating them, and verifying proofs.

Rules
  • Be respectful — no harmful, offensive, or NSFW content.
  • Submit genuine proof — fake submissions may lead to account action.
  • Challenge creators have final say on approval/rejection.
  • Keep challenges fun and safe — no dangerous stunts!
  • Streaks reset if you skip a day without completing a challenge.
