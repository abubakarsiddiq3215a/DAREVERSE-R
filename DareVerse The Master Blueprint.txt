DareVerse: The Master Blueprint
This document is the final consolidated plan for the DareVerse MVP monetization, gamification, and safety systems.

1. Creator Eligibility & Licenses
The Grind: Users must complete 25 Public Dares to host a Prize Pool challenge.
The Express Lane: Users/Brands can pay a "Creator License Fee" (e.g., ₹499) to skip the 25-dare requirement and start hosting immediately.
Creator Dashboard: Tools for hosts to review, approve, or reject (mark as "Fake") submissions.
2. Challenge Economics (In-App Money)
Minimum Prize Pool: ₹100.
Platform Commission: 10% taken from the Prize Pool during creation.
Escrow System: App holds the pool money safely until the 7-day deadline expires and winners are verified.
3. Continuous Creator Rewards (The Payout)
The app pays creators for every verified participant they bring in.

Model: 80/20 Revenue Split on the bonus rate.
Small Pools (₹100+): ₹0.60 Net per participant.
Medium Pools (₹1,000+): ₹1.20 Net per participant.
Mega Pools (₹5,000+): ₹2.40 Net per participant.
Funding: These bonuses are paid for by the Ad Revenue generated by the challenge views.
4. Winner Payouts & Withdrawals
Withdrawal Minimum: ₹100.
Processing Fee: A flat ₹5 - ₹10 fee per withdrawal to cover bank costs.
Approval: Manual Admin approval for all withdrawals (Safety Phase).
Methods: UPI (VPA) and Direct Bank Transfer.
Pending Wallet: Earnings are visible immediately but "Locked" for a 24-hour security audit before they can be withdrawn.
5. Prestige & Reward System
Digital Ranks (Based on Points/XP)
Levels: 10 Ranks from Rookie to Dare Legend (1,000,000 pts).
Points: Earned via Dares (+50), Wins (+100), and Social Sharing (+10, max 50/day).
Physical Trophies (The "Play Buttons" & "Mastery" Series)
Sent to the user's home for FREE (No shipping fees). Based on PUBLIC HARD Dares + Wins.

Track A: Global Play Buttons (Platform Dominance)
Bronze: 550 Hard Dares + 10 Wins.
Silver: 1,100 Hard Dares + 20 Wins.
Gold: 2,200 Hard Dares + 40 Wins.
Diamond: 4,400 Hard Dares + 80 Wins.
Track B: Domain Mastery (Specialist Trophies)
Requirement: 250 Hard Dares + 5 Wins within a single domain.

The Titan (Fitness): Industrial Steel aesthetic.
The Apex (Gaming): Cyber-Neon Acrylic.
The Maestro (Creative): Art-Glass.
The Scholar (Education): Brass & Wood.
The Star (Entertainment): Chrome & Gold.
The Diplomat (Social): Crystal Geometrics.
The Knight (Honor): Matte Silver Shield.
The Chef (Food): Marble & Copper.
6. VIP Subscription (Monthly)
Status: Gold Badge and "Verified" icon.
Spy Power: Ability to see the Total Career Earnings of any other user (Total only, not individual breakdown).
Privacy: VIPs do not get a discount on commission—everyone stays equal.
7. Community Safety (The Shield)
Content Rules: Zero tolerance for harmful, abusive, religious, insulting, or suicidal content.
The Mandatory Example: Every creator MUST upload an "Example Proof" video. This acts as the Ground Truth for the AI and a visual guide for players.
AI Verification (The Counter): AI uses the Creator Example to learn the "Pattern" but counts individual repetitions to ensure the target (e.g. 100 reps) is met.
The 3-Strike Rule:
Strike 1: 1-week ban + Reset Streak.
Strike 2: Full App Ban + Reset Streak.
Strike 3: Account Termination.
8. UX & Marketing Mechanics
Reels Feed: Vertical scrolling video proofs with a "DO THIS DARE" button.
Submission Race: Top 5 winners logic based on "First-Come, First-Served" timestamp queue.
Submission Ads: 5-10 second ads displayed during the video upload process.
Profile Portfolio: A grid of all completed/won dares for every user.
Deadlines: Every challenge has a 24h, 7d, or 30d countdown timer.
9. Native Ad & Sponsorship Guidelines
The "Native" Rule: All ads must use the DareVerse CSS Design System (Neon, Glassmorphism, Bebas Neue font).
Campaign Labeling: Ads are labeled as "SPONSORED DARE" or "CAMPAIGN" to maintain prestige.
Placement: Feed-native cards (every 7th), Upload Interstitials, and Rewarded "Double XP" Dares.
10. Animation & Motion Design System
Motion is the soul of the app. All actions must feel high-end and cinematic using GSAP.

Core Engine: GSAP (GreenSock) + CSS Particles.
Key Motion Profiles:
The "Electric Snap": Flash overlay + elastic scale for accepting/creating dares.
The "Liquid Meter": Vertical neon energy fill for proof uploads.
The "Staggered Cascade": Sequential row entry for Leaderboards.
The "Cyber Spotlight": Blackout + 3D trophy reveal for winners.
The "Tumble Count": Slot-machine style count-up with coin rain for payouts.
The "Digital Glitch": Color-shift dissolve for closing challenges.
