# DareVerse — Points System & Rules Guidelines

Welcome to **DareVerse**, the ultimate arena for challenging friends, proving your skills, and rising through the ranks. This document outlines exactly how the progression system works, how to earn Experience Points (XP), maintain streaks, and claim achievement badges.

---

## 1. Earning Experience Points (XP)

Your standing on the scoreboard is determined by your total XP. You can earn XP through the following actions:

### Challenge Completion
*   **Easy Dares**: **+10 XP**
*   **Medium Dares**: **+25 XP**
*   **Hard Dares**: **+50 XP**
*   **Honor Dares** (No media upload required): **+5 XP**

### Progression Bonuses
*   **Early Bird Bonus**: **+15 XP** if you are among the first **10 players** to complete any challenge.
*   **Creator Popularity Bonus**: If you create a challenge, you receive **+20 XP** when 10 players conquer it, and **+50 XP** when 50 players conquer it.

### Platform Engagement
*   **Create a Challenge**: **+5 XP**
*   **Verify a Friend's Proof** (as the challenge creator): **+2 XP**
*   **Share a Challenge to WhatsApp**: **+2 XP**
*   **Vote on a Public Community Challenge Proof**: **+1 XP**

---

## 2. Daily Streaks

Consistency is rewarded in DareVerse. Complete a challenge each day to grow your active streak:

*   Completing a dare on consecutive days grows your streak count.
*   **5-Day Streak Bonus**: **+30 XP**
*   **10-Day Streak Bonus**: **+75 XP**
*   If you skip a day, your active streak resets back to 1.

## 4. Achievement Badges Cabinet

Unlock permanent showcase badges on your profile by hitting specific milestones:

*   **First Blood**: Complete your very first challenge.
*   **On Fire**: Maintain a daily challenge streak of **7 days**.
*   **Unstoppable**: Complete **10 Hard difficulty** challenges.
*   **Socialite**: Grow your friends circle to **10 friends**.
*   **Creator**: Host and post **5 challenges**.
*   **Verifier**: Review and approve **5 proofs** submitted to your challenges.
*   **Domain Master**: Complete **10 challenges** inside a single category (e.g. Fitness, Gaming, Creative).
*   **Social Butterfly**: Share **5 challenges** to WhatsApp.

---

## 5. Verification Rules & Public Challenges

How your proof of dare completion is verified depends on the type of challenge:

### A. Friends-Only (Private) Challenges
*   When you submit a proof (image or video), it goes to the **Challenge Creator's** inbox.
*   The creator manually reviews it and can either **Approve** it (awarding you full completion XP) or **Reject** it.

### B. Public Community Challenges
*   When you submit a proof, it goes to the **Global Standings** list for community voting.
*   Other players vote on whether the proof is **Valid** or **Invalid**.
*   **Resolution Thresholds**:
    *   Once a proof receives **at least 5 votes**:
        *   If approval rate is **70% or higher** $\rightarrow$ **Approved** (XP awarded).
        *   If approval rate is **less than 40%** $\rightarrow$ **Rejected** (no XP awarded).
        *   If it is between 40% and 70% $\rightarrow$ **Escalated** to the creator for final judgment.
